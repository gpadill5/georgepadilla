## Current work

- **Research Fellow in Materials Science and Engineering at Cornell University** at [CAPE CryStAL](https://capecrystal.mse.cornell.edu/).

- Building **Python machine-learning pipelines for crystal-structure classification** from molecular dynamics trajectories. [Project](/project/crystal-classifier/).

- Modeling **active fluids** using hybrid molecular dynamics + Monte Carlo simulations, and implementing unsupervised ML techniques to label liquid phases.

## Selected projects & notes

- [Crystal-Structure Classification from MD Self-Assembled Crystals](/project/crystal-classifier/)  
  Identifying self-assembled crystal structures and grains directly from molecular-dynamics snapshots.

- [Computational Modeling of Active Fluids](/project/active-fluids/)  
  Molecular dynamics and Monte Carlo simulations of Lennard-Jones binary fluids with particle interconversion.

- [Estimating Pi by Dropping Disks](/blog/pi-disk-drop/)  
  A small Monte Carlo demo that estimates pi from random disks landing inside an outlined circle.
